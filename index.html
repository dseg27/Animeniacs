ABOUT

Anime is increasing in popularity, and there are so many great shows to explore on platforms that you already watch live-action shows on-- Netflix, Hulu, Amazon, and Disney Plus.

Don't know where to start?

There is no well-known resource currently available to recommend new anime series. We're here to display trends and help you pick one to start watching!

We will use machine learning and sentence embedding to compare the synopses of live action shows with animes, to recommend the best animes for our viewers.
